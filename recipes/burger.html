Description

Burgers are the quintessential crowd-pleaser, combining juicy, seasoned patties with fresh toppings, melted cheese, and a toasted bun. Whether you prefer a simple cheeseburger or a gourmet creation, this handheld meal is endlessly customizable and perfect for any occasion, from casual cookouts to quick weeknight dinners.

This recipe focuses on crafting the perfect burger—one that's flavorful, juicy, and satisfying. Paired with your choice of toppings and condiments, it's a guaranteed hit for burger lovers everywhere.

Ingredients

For the Patties

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef, 80/20 lean-to-fat ratio recommended
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce (optional, for extra flavor)

For Assembly

  • 4 burger buns, toasted
  • 4 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, Swiss, or your favorite)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices (raw or caramelized)
  • Pickles
  • condiments: ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, or your favorite sauce

Optional Additions

  • Avocado slices
  • Fried eggs
  • Bacon strips
  • Sautéed mushrooms
