Home

Description

Shawarma is a flavorful Middle Eastern dish that has won hearts worldwide with its tender, spiced meat and irresistible aroma. Traditionally cooked on a rotating spit, shawarma features marinated meat—such as chicken, beef, or lamb—thinly sliced and served in warm pita bread or flatbread, along with fresh vegetables, pickles, and creamy sauces like tahini or garlic aioli.

This recipe adapts the classic technique for home cooking, capturing the essence of shawarma’s bold flavors and vibrant accompaniments. Perfect for a quick meal or a crowd-pleasing feast, shawarma is as versatile as it is delicious.

Ingredients

For the Marinade

  • 2 pounds (900g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper(optional, for heat)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Assembly

  • 6-8 pita breads or flatbreads
  • 1 cup tahini sauce or garlic sauce
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced onions, optional
  • 1/2 cup sliced pickles
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Optional Additions

  • Hummus
  • Grilled vegetables
  • Hot sauce
